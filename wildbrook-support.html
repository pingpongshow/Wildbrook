Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset the app?

To completely reset an app:

  1. Delete the app from your device (tap and hold the app icon, then select "Remove App")
  2. Reinstall it from the App Store

All data is stored locally on your device, so deleting the app removes all associated data.

Do I need an account to use Wildbrook apps?

No! None of our apps require account creation. Everything works directly on your device with no sign-up needed.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All Wildbrook apps keep your data on your device only. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I sync data between devices?

Some apps may offer iCloud sync. If available, you can enable it in the app settings. Your data syncs through your personal iCloud account - we never have access to it.

The app crashed or isn't working correctly

Try these steps:

  1. Force close the app (swipe up from the bottom and swipe the app away)
  2. Restart your device
  3. Make sure you have the latest version of the app from the App Store
  4. Check that your iOS is up to date (Settings > General > Software Update)

If the problem persists, please email us with details about what you were doing when it happened.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed through Apple. To request a refund:

  1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find the purchase and select "Report a Problem"
  4. Choose "Request a refund" and follow the prompts

You can also contact Apple Support directly for refund requests.

How do I manage permissions (Camera, Location, Photos)?

To change app permissions:

  1. Open Settings on your device
  2. Scroll down and find the app name
  3. Toggle permissions on or off as needed

Remember: All permissions are optional. You can deny them if you don't need those features.

Can I export my data?

Most of our apps include sharing or export features. Look for the share button (square with arrow) within the app to export your data in standard formats.

I have a feature request or suggestion

We love hearing from users! Please email us at wildbrookllc@icloud.com with your ideas. We read every message.

Before Contacting Support

To help us assist you quickly, please include:

  • App name and version (found in App Store listing or app settings)
  • Device model (e.g., iPhone 14 Pro, iPad Air)
  • iOS version (Settings > General > About)
  • Detailed description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Screenshots if applicable (helps us understand the issue)

Response Time

We typically respond to support emails within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent issues, please mention "URGENT" in your subject line.
